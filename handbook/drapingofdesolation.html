Draping of Desolation

An ancient incantation that puts a shroud of desolation over a small area, withering all life within its grasp and transforming fertile fields into barren wastelands.

To Cast This Spell:

  • Click the "Begin Incantation" button below
  • Speak clearly: "Draping of Desolation"
  • The spell will activate when the words are recognized
  • You may need to grant microphone permissions
