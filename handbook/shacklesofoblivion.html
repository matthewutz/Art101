Shackles of Oblivion

An ancient incantation that weaves ethereal shackles that bind the target and drag them into a void dimension, where they remain trapped in eternal darkness and isolation.

To Cast This Spell:

  • Click the "Begin Incantation" button below
  • Speak clearly: "Shackles of Oblivion"
  • The spell will activate when the words are recognized
  • You may need to grant microphone permissions
