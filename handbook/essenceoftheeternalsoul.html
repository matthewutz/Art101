Essence of the Eternal Soul

An ancient incantation that binds the very essence of eternal existence into the caster's soul, granting them unending life that transcends the boundaries of time itself.

To Cast This Spell:

  • Click the "Begin Incantation" button below
  • Speak clearly: "Essence of the Eternal Soul"
  • The spell will activate when the words are recognized
  • You may need to grant microphone permissions
