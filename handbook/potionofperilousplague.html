Potion of Perilous Plague

A deadly potion that turns the consumer into the patient zero of a magical virus that causes weakness and disorientation.

Brewing Instructions:

Required Ingredients:

  • 5 Bat Wings
  • 2 Vials of Blood
  • 3 Tears of a Banshee
  • Click on ingredients below to add them to the cauldron
  • Add the exact amounts shown in the recipe
  • Once all ingredients are added, click "Brew Potion"
  • Beware, incorrect amounts may cause death
