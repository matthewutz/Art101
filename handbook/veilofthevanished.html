Veil of the Vanished

An ancient incantation that obscures the victim from mortal eyes and from memory itself, effectively disolving them from the collective consciousness of the world.

To Cast This Spell:

  • Click the "Begin Incantation" button below
  • Speak clearly: "Veil of the Vanished"
  • The spell will activate when the words are recognized
  • You may need to grant microphone permissions
