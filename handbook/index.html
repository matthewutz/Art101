The Spellcaster's Handbook of Insurrection

The Spellcaster's Handbook of Insurrection was a sacred text, whose hallowed pages of potions and incantations harbored the potential for unfathomable malevolence and destruction. Once believed to be lost to the annals of time, this arcane tome was recently unearthed, and its ancient wisdom is currently being meticulously transcribed into the digital realm. We hope that you are merely a scholar whose intentions are pure, and whose pursuits are noble, as the secrets contained in this book possess the capacity for great evils that could unleash a wave of chaos upon an unsuspecting world.
